Are your snorkeling tours beginner-friendly?

Absolutely! Our tours are perfect for beginners and experienced snorkelers alike. We provide a short training session before the tour and our guides are always there to assist you in the water.

Is snorkeling safe in Mirissa?

Yes, it's very safe! We use high-quality gear, and our professional guides ensure your safety at all times. Life jackets are also available for those who prefer extra support.

Do I need to bring my own snorkeling gear?

No need! We provide all necessary snorkeling equipment, including masks, fins, snorkels, and life jackets. You just need to bring your swimwear and a towel.

About Us

Mirissa, located on Sri Lanka’s stunning south coast, is one of the best places for snorkeling. The calm, clear waters are home to vibrant coral reefs and gentle sea turtles that make every dive magical. Whether you’re a first-timer or a pro, this is the perfect spot to explore life under the waves.
